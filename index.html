Zane Flynt

The Operative

Action Skill

Digi-Clone (a holographic twin that shoots and swaps places with you), SNTNL (an attack drone buddy), and the Barrier (a deployable shield). Uniquely, Zane can equip TWO of these at once — he just gives up grenades to do it.

Backstory

Born on Pandora into the infamous Flynt family — brother of Captain Flynt and Baron Flynt. Spent decades as a corporate assassin before "retiring" into Vault hunting. Talks constantly, mostly to himself, sometimes to his clone.

Playstyle

Trickster. Confuse enemies with the clone, zip around the battlefield, and let your gadgets do half the work while you do all the talking.

Fl4k

The Beastmaster

Action Skill

Fl4k can summon and command pets to fight alongside him, each with unique abilities. He can also enter a powerful "Fade Away" mode, becoming invisible and dealing massive damage.

Backstory

Fl4k is a mysterious creature hunter from the wilds of Pandora. Raised by a pack of robotic creatures, he has a deep bond with his pets and a unique perspective on the world.

Playstyle

Pet Master. Command your pets to attack, distract, and support you in battle while you stay at a safe distance, using your unique abilities to control the flow of combat.

Ellie

NPC - Mechanic

Role in the Game

Ellie is the chief mechanic of Sanctuary III, providing players with vehicle upgrades, repairs, and customizations. She also offers side quests and valuable information about the world of Pandora.

Backstory

Ellie has been a skilled mechanic for years, known for her expertise in vehicles and machinery. She has a big heart and a passion for helping others, often putting herself at risk to assist Vault Hunters.

Personality

Ellie is tough, resourceful, and fiercely independent. She has a no-nonsense attitude but also shows compassion and loyalty to those she trusts.

Moze

The Gunner

Role in the Game

Moze is a versatile combatant, capable of dealing massive damage with her arsenal of weapons and her trusty mech, Iron Bear. She excels in both offensive and defensive roles, making her a valuable asset in any team.

Backstory

Moze has a military background and a strong sense of duty. She joined the fight against the Crimson Raiders to protect her home and loved ones, using her skills and Iron Bear to turn the tide of battle.

Personality

Moze is determined, courageous, and fiercely loyal to her friends and allies. She has a strong sense of justice and is always ready to face any challenge head-on.

Amara

The Siren

Role in the Game

Amara is a powerful Siren, capable of harnessing mystical abilities to control the battlefield. She excels in both offensive and defensive roles, making her a versatile and formidable character.

Backstory

Amara has trained extensively to master her Siren abilities, seeking to protect those who cannot protect themselves. Her journey is one of self-discovery and empowerment, as she learns to harness her powers for the greater good.

Personality

Amara is confident, determined, and compassionate. She is driven by a strong sense of justice and a desire to help those in need, always striving to make a positive impact on the world around her.

Cl4pTrap

NPC - Robot

Role in the Game

Cl4pTrap is a quirky and unpredictable robot, known for his humorous antics and explosive combat abilities. He provides comic relief while also being a formidable ally in battle.

Backstory

Cl4pTrap was created as a general-purpose robot, but his unique personality and penchant for chaos set him apart. He has a mysterious past and a knack for getting into trouble, often finding himself in the middle of unexpected adventures.

Personality

Cl4pTrap is eccentric, unpredictable, and full of energy. He has a mischievous sense of humor and enjoys causing chaos, but he is also loyal and will go to great lengths to help his friends.
